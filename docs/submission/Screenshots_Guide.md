## Capture Checklist

- Browser zoom at 100%
- Full page without scroll overlap
- Hide personal secrets/tokens
- Use realistic seeded data
- Keep consistent theme and resolution
- Prefer 1366x768 or 1920x1080 for desktop screenshots
